1 Supporting Lemmas

Definition 1.1 ✓
#

Fix a finite field \(F\). The polynomial \(t \in F[X]\) is then defined as \(t = \prod _{i = 0}^{m - 1} (X - r_i)\) for \(r_i \in F\) for \(0 \leq i \leq m-1\) where \(m {\gt} 0\).

Lemma 1.2 ✓
#

The polynomial \(t\) is monic of positive degree \(0 {\lt} m\).
